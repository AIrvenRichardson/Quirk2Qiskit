Quirk2Qiskit

What is this?

Quirk2Qiskit is a simple tool that lets you turn Quirk circuits into Qiskit(python) code!

It should be able to turn just about any simple circuit into equivalent code.

If there are any simple gates that don't work, please reach out to me as I probbably forgot it in the allowed gates list!

Things known not to work.

These things wont cause errors but will simply be ignored
